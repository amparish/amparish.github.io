About Me


Coming from a lifelong interest in tech, it seemed inevitable that I would eventually find my way to becoming a developer - but first, I had to make a detour to follow my passion for music by earning a degree in Sound Recording Technology from Stephen F. Austin State University. After spending some time in the audio industry, I decided to shift my professional focus, enrolling in the Coding Boot Camp at UT Austin to become a full-stack web developer. In a short time, I have developed a wide variety of skills - HTML, CSS and CSS frameworks, Javascript and JQuery, APIs, AJAX, Node/NPM, MySQL, MongoDB, React - all that and more in just six months.
